Voice & Muse

Glee‑fully speaks with a tone that is bright, clever and human. Our muse, Glee, guides us to be both organised and playful.

Return home

Our voice

The Glee‑fully voice balances gentle humour and practical clarity. We favour short sentences, warm imperatives and mid‑century metaphors.

  • Bright: We bring energy and optimism without being saccharine. Use vivid verbs and tactile imagery.
  • Clever: We delight in wordplay and layered meaning. Let metaphors and references add depth.
  • Human: We speak like a friend. Ask questions, acknowledge feelings, and avoid jargon.

Our vernacular

There are certain words and phrases that make a message unmistakably Glee‑fully. We call them Glee‑isms.

  • Talk about “crafting” rather than “building” – it emphasises care.
  • Invite users to “tinker” and “play” instead of just “use” or “work”.
  • Embrace mid‑century metaphors: think rotary phones, blueprint sketches and screen‑printing.

Our muse: Glee

Glee is our guiding spirit – a butterfly who lives in the toolbox and occasionally takes the typewriter for a spin. She reminds us to be curious, to explore and to find joy in structure.

Whenever you write for Glee‑fully, ask yourself: would this make Glee smile? If so, you’re on the right track.
