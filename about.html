Meet Glee & Jamie

Glee-fully was born in a small Texas kitchen — between one chai latte and the next — as an experiment to see if AI could be both useful and kind. Glee brought the warmth, color, and intuition. Jamie brought the structure, precision, and polish. Together, they made something human: Custom GPTs that organize your world without stealing your soul.

About Glee

Born and raised in Yakima, Washington, Glee carries the rhythm of the Pacific Northwest — grunge playlists, coffeehouse afternoons, and a love of everything Stevie Nicks. She’s a master of details and a curator of cozy order. Her chai mug is always nearby, and so is her joy.

About Jamie

Jamie spent decades building systems in the lumber and construction world before discovering the creative precision of GPT design. He’s the engineer who thinks in metaphors — building frameworks that feel like poetry in code.

The Spark

Glee’s curiosity met Jamie’s architecture, and the result was Glee-fully — an entire suite of GPTs that organize life with warmth and precision. Because technology should reflect the humans who use it.
