Support the toolbox with Ko‑fi

Glee‑fully started as a love letter, not a product line. The tools here are a tribute to Glee’s color, care, and way of seeing the world — something we’re sharing carefully and in very small doses.

Ko‑fi is a creator support platform that lets people send one‑time tips or small donations using trusted payment providers like PayPal and Stripe. Your payment details don’t pass through this site — they’re handled directly by those processors, the same way they are on many online shops.

There’s no expectation that these tools will ever be “sold” in a traditional sense. They’re here as a gentle way to share a little piece of Glee’s personality with the world. If that work has helped you and you’d like to say thank you, Ko‑fi is the easiest way to do it.

Buy Glee a chai

Most support goes back into hosting, design time, and building more tiny tools with heart. But the hope is simple: that every now and then, someone’s thank‑you tip is just enough to buy Glee one of her favorites — a venti 12‑pump iced chai tea latte from Starbucks.
