This page explains, in straightforward terms, what Glee‑fully Personalizable Tools™ is, how it should be used, and how it relates to sibling projects like AskJamie™ and OverKill Hill P³™.

Scope of this notice

This notice applies to the glee-fully.tools website, the Glee‑fully Personalizable Tools™ brand, and the public GPT experiences, “Tools” and “Tool‑ettes” that are linked from this site (collectively, the “Glee‑fully Tools”).

Sibling projects — including AskJamie™ and other OverKill Hill P³™ experiments — may reference or interoperate with Glee‑fully, but may also publish additional terms specific to their own spaces.

Not legal, financial, clinical, or other regulated advice

Glee‑fully Tools are assistive and exploratory. Nothing in these tools, prompts, diagrams, or conversations should be treated as legal, financial, medical, therapeutic, or other regulated professional advice.

You are responsible for verifying information, checking facts against authoritative sources, and consulting qualified human professionals where appropriate.

Experimental, evolving systems

All GPT configurations, workflows, examples, and diagrams are experimental and subject to change. Outputs may be incomplete, incorrect, outdated, or misaligned with your context.

Use of these systems is at your discretion and risk. You should not rely on any single output for decisions that carry legal, financial, safety, compliance, or health impact.

Your responsibilities as a user

When you use Glee‑fully Tools, you agree to:

  • Apply your own judgment before acting on any recommendation or plan.
  • Cross‑check important facts and numbers against primary sources or trusted professionals.
  • Respect confidentiality obligations and avoid pasting in information you are not entitled to share.
  • Use the tools only in ways that comply with applicable laws, regulations, organizational policies, and platform terms of service.

Data, privacy, and third‑party platforms

The public Glee‑fully website is currently a static, informational site. There is no account system, login, or custom user database operated directly from this domain.

When you click out to a Glee‑fully Tool hosted on a third‑party platform (for example, a Custom GPT on ChatGPT), your interaction is governed by that platform’s own terms of use and privacy policy. Glee‑fully does not control how those platforms store or process your data.

If you email the studio or otherwise contact us directly, we will of course see the information you choose to share and may retain it for the purposes of responding, improving the tools, or handling support.

Generative AI limitations & acceptable use

Glee‑fully Tools and AskJamie™ are built on large language models provided by third parties. These models can hallucinate, misread prompts, or surface unwanted biases.

By using these tools, you agree that you will not use them to:

  • Generate or distribute hate, harassment, exploitation, or targeted abuse.
  • Promote violence, self‑harm, or unlawful activity.
  • Create intentionally deceptive or harmful misinformation.

You remain responsible for how outputs are applied in real‑world contexts.

Intellectual property

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, all content, structures, naming conventions, and visual systems associated with Glee‑fully Personalizable Tools™, AskJamie™, and OverKill Hill P³™ are proprietary intellectual property of OverKill Hill P³™.

You may reference and experiment with the ideas for personal or exploratory use. You may not resell, rebrand, or present significant portions of the system as your own without written permission.

Underlying AI models and platforms (for example, services operated by OpenAI or other providers) remain the property of their respective owners and are subject to their own terms.
