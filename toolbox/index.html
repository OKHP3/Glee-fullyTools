Your joyful command center for every Glee‑fully Tool.

It introduces the trunk‑branch‑twig‑leaf structure, helps you choose the right helper for today’s task, and sends you straight into that GPT with context.

Open the Toolbox GPT See the ecosystem map

What the Toolbox GPT actually does

Think of the Toolbox as the friendly front desk for the whole Glee‑fully suite. It doesn’t replace the other GPTs — it makes them easier to find, understand, and reuse.

Explains the ecosystem

The Toolbox walks you through the Trunk → Tools → Tool‑ettes → Functions structure so you always know where you are — and where to go next.

Routes you to the right GPT

Describe what you’re trying to do — “plan a trip,” “organize bills,” “log my books” — and the Toolbox recommends and opens the Tool or Tool‑ette best suited for the job.

Acts as suite‑aware concierge

With a shared index of all Tools and Tool‑ettes, the Toolbox can remember where you’ve been, suggest next stops, and keep tone and structure consistent across the whole experience.

Why start here instead of vanilla ChatGPT?

Generic ChatGPT is powerful but general‑purpose. The Toolbox is tuned around one person’s life — Glee’s — and by extension, yours.

Suite‑aware, not single‑session

The Toolbox understands how all seven Tools connect, so it won’t reinvent a structure that already exists elsewhere in the suite.

Template‑first by design

Instead of loose chat, it leans on ready‑to‑copy tables, trackers, and checklists you can drop into docs, sheets, or Notion.

Dialable tone & “BLEED GLEE” mode

You can ask the Toolbox to turn the charm up (BLEED GLEE) or keep things neutral and professional — no prompt‑engineering required.

How Toolbox, Tools, and Tool‑ettes fit together
